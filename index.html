What We Offer

Comprehensive Gynaecological care

At Lima's Health Hub, we provide comprehensive gynecological care tailored to meet the unique needs of women at every stage of life. Our experienced team offers routine check-ups, preventive screenings, and treatment for a wide range of gynecological conditions. We are committed to ensuring your reproductive health and overall well-being with personalized care plans and compassionate support.

Obsteric Services

Our obstetric services are designed to support you throughout your pregnancy journey. From preconception counseling to postpartum care, our dedicated team of obstetricians and midwives is here to provide expert guidance and support. We offer prenatal care, ultrasound imaging, labor and delivery services, and postpartum follow-up to ensure a healthy and positive experience for both mother and baby.

Fertility Treatment

Lima's Health Hub offers advanced fertility treatments to help you achieve your dream of starting or expanding your family. Our fertility specialists provide a range of services, including fertility assessments, in vitro fertilization (IVF), intrauterine insemination (IUI), and fertility preservation. We are committed to offering compassionate care and the latest reproductive technologies to support your fertility journey.

Pediatrics Care

Our pediatric care services ensure that your child receives the best possible medical attention from infancy through adolescence. Our pediatricians provide routine check-ups, immunizations, developmental screenings, and treatment for common childhood illnesses. We strive to create a welcoming and child-friendly environment to make healthcare visits a positive experience for both children and parents.

Preventive Health Screenings

Preventive health screenings are essential for early detection and management of various health conditions. We offer a range of screenings, including blood pressure checks, cholesterol tests, diabetes screenings, and cancer screenings. Our goal is to help you maintain optimal health through regular monitoring and preventive care.
